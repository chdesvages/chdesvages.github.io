Identify \(S^{2m+1}\) with the subspace

\begin{equation*} \{w \in \CC^{n+1} : \|w\|=1\} \subset \CC^{n+1}\setminus \{0\}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Write \(h\) for the composite map

\begin{equation*} S^{2n+1} \subset \CC^{n+1} \setminus \{0\} \to \PP^n_{\CC}\text{,} \end{equation*}

and identify the fibers \(h^{-1}[z]\text{,}\) up to homeomorphism.
