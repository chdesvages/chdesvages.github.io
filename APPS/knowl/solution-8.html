Solution 4.5.1.1.

We may suppose \(|a|\leq |b|\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} ab\leq |ab|=|a|\cdot|b|\leq |b|\cdot |b|=|b|^2=b^2= \max\{a^2,b^2\}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Similarly,

\begin{equation*} ab\geq -|ab|=-|a|\cdot |b| \geq -|b|^2=-b^2=- \max\{a^2,b^2\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
