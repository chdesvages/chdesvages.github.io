Let \(S\) be a set. A total ordering \(\lt\) on \(S\) is a relation \(\lt\) thereon satisfying the following axioms:

  1. Transitivity. For every \(x,y,z\in S\text{,}\) if \(x\lt y\) and \(y\lt z\text{,}\) then \(x\lt z\text{.}\)

  2. Trichotomy. For every \(x,y \in S\text{,}\) exactly one of the following is true: \(x=y\text{,}\) \(x\lt y\text{,}\) or \(x\gt y\text{.}\)
