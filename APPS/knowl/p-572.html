Let \((x_{n})\) and \((y_{n})\) be sequences.

  1. If \(x_{n}\rightarrow x\text{,}\) \(y_{n}\rightarrow y\text{,}\) and \(x_{n}\leq y_{n}\) for all \(n\in\mathbb{N}\text{,}\) then \(x\leq y\text{.}\)

  2. If \(x_n\rightarrow x\) and \(x_{n}\leq y\) for all \(n\text{,}\) then \(x\leq y\text{.}\)
