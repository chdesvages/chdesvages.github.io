Let \(n_0 \in \NN_0\text{,}\) and for each natural number \(n \geq n_0\text{,}\) let \(\phi(n)\) be a statement. Then the statement \((\forall n\in\NN_0)((n\geq n_0)\implies \phi(n))\) is logically equivalent to the conjunction of the following statements:

  • \(\phi(n_0)\text{;}\)

  • \((\forall n\in\NN_0)(((n\geq n_0)\wedge(\forall k\in\NN_0)((k\leq n)\implies\phi(k)))\implies \phi(n+1))\text{.}\)
