For part (a), the fact that \(|a+b\sqrt{-5}|=\sqrt{a^{2}+5b^{2}}\) juts follows from the definition of the modulus of a complex number. In particular, if \(z=a+b\sqrt{-5}\) has modulus 1, then \(1=|z|^{2}=a^{2}+5b^{2}\text{,}\) and the only way this can happen is if \(b=0\) and \(a=1\) (since a and b are integers).
