  • real numbers equipped with \(+\text{,}\)

  • non-zero real numbers equipped with \(\cdot\) (i.e. multiplication),

  • complex numbers equipped with \(+\text{,}\)

  • non-zero complex numbers equipped with \(\cdot\text{,}\)

  • \(n\)-th root of unity equipped with \(\cdot\text{,}\)
