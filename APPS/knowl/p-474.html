To begin the iteration, we set \(r_0 = b\) and \(r_1 = a\text{.}\) To construct the rest of the sequence, assume that for some \(k\in\N\) we have constructed nonnegative integers \(r_k \lt r_{k-1} \lt \dots \lt r_1 \lt r_0\) with the two properties above. Now use the Remainder Theorem to find an integers \(q_{k+1}\) and \(r_{k+1}\) such that

\begin{equation*} r_{k-1} = q_{k+1}r_k + r_{k+1}\text{.} \end{equation*}
