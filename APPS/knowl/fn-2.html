Footnote 3.4.2.
It is perhaps disconcerting that it's not possible to prove the Archimedean property only using the rules (A0-A4) and (M0-M5), together with (R1-R5) which are introduced in Section 3. Indeed, it can be established that, in the terminology of Section 3, there are ordered fields which do not satisfy the Archimedean property. It is precisely because of pitfalls such as this that we are adopting a careful axiomatic perspective for the real numbers.
