Definition 15.4.2.

Given \(n \in \mathbb{N}\text{,}\) \(k \in \mathbb{N}\) with \(k \geq 2\text{,}\) and nonnegative integers \(r_{1}, \dots ,r_{k}\) such that \(r_{1}+\cdots + r_{k}=n\text{,}\) we denote the number of ordered partitions \((A_{1}, \dots ,A_{k})\) of a set \(S\) such that \(|A_{i}|=r_{i}\) by

\begin{equation*} {n \choose r_{1}, \dots , r_{k}}\text{.} \end{equation*}
