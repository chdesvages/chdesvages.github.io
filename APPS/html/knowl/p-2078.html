We use symbol \(\iota\) (the Greek letter “iota” ) to denote the identity permutation

\begin{equation*} \iota = \left(\begin{matrix}1 \amp 2 \amp 3 \amp 4 \amp \ldots \amp n \cr1 \amp 2 \amp 3 \amp 4 \amp \ldots \amp n\cr \end{matrix} \right), \;\;\; \iota(x) =x \;\;\mbox{ for all } x\in \{1,2,...,n\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
