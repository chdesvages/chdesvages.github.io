For every permutation \(f\in\mathrm{S}_n\text{,}\) let \(f=\sigma_{1}\sigma_{2}\cdots\sigma_{s}\) where \(\sigma_{1},\ldots,\sigma_{s}\) are disjoint cycles of lengths \(r_{1},\ldots,r_{s}\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \mathrm{sgn}(f)=(-1)^{r_1-1}(-1)^{r_2-1}(-1)^{r_3-1}\cdots (-1)^{r_s-1}\text{.} \end{equation*}
