Let \(n\in\mathbb{N}\) and \(x_{1}, \dots ,x_{k}\in \mathbb{C}\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation} (x_{1}+\cdots + x_{k})^{n} = \sum {n\choose r_{1},\dots ,r_{k}} x_1^{r_{1}}\cdots x_{k}^{r_{k}}\tag{15.5.2} \end{equation}

where the sum is taken over all \(r_{i}\in \{0,1, \dots ,n\}\) such that \(r_{1}+\cdots + r_{k}=n\text{.}\)
